Progressive Lenses

Progressive lenses are the true "multifocal" lenses that provide a smooth, seamless progression of many lens powers for clear vision across all distances without excessive head movements. They resemble more closely the natural vision before presbyopia occurred.

Transitions Lenses

Transitions lenses offer the best light control technology for double protection against UV and glare. Improved temperature stability and responsiveness of the latest series allows only the right amount of light to go through to best enhance your vision in all outdoor conditions.
